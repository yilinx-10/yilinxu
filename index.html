A computational social scientist and data analyst, presently at large in Chicago. My work lives at the intersection of statistics, computation, and the social world—I build instruments to measure things that are difficult to measure, and tell stories that the data alone cannot quite tell.

I am drawn to questions about culture, belief, and institutions: How do people across sixty-six countries think about science? What does it mean when a large language model "understands" a culture—and, more interestingly, when does it fail? My implements include multilevel models, NLP pipelines, and interactive visualisations—but the question, always, comes first.

Outside of research I care a great deal about clear communication: making complex findings feel quite accessible, without sacrificing the nuance.

计算社会科学研究者与数据分析师，现居芝加哥。工作处于统计、计算与社会世界的交汇处——构建能够测量难以测量之物的工具，讲述数据本身无法独自讲述的故事。

我尤其关注文化、信念与制度方面的问题：六十六个国家的人们如何看待科学？当一个大语言模型"理解"一种文化时，这意味着什么——更有趣的是，它在何处失败？工具包括多层次模型、NLP流程与交互式可视化，但问题永远先于工具。

研究之外，我格外重视清晰的沟通——让复杂的发现变得易于理解，同时不失其细腻。

Mapping Belief Networks Across Nations

绘制跨国信念网络之图谱

Graph-based representations of cultural belief systems — Content v.s. Structure.

以图结构呈现文化信念体系 —— 特征工程与分类模型的相遇。

Constructed graph-based representations of cultural belief systems from 100k+ survey responses with Python and NetworkX. Engineered features from network structure (centrality, community detection) and trained classification models to predict cultural cluster membership. Findings are presented through an interactive cross-filtered dashboard built in D3.js & Svelte, mapping representational deviations across the Inglehart–Welzel cultural dimensions.

基于 NetworkX，从 10 万余条调查样本中构建文化信念体系的图结构表示。提取网络特征（中心性、社区检测）作为机器学习输入，训练分类模型预测文化聚类归属。研究发现通过基于 D3.js 与 Svelte 构建的交互式交叉过滤仪表盘呈现，将表征偏差映射至英格哈特–韦尔策尔文化维度。

NLP & ETL at Scale大规模 NLP 1M+ Posts百万级数据

What 1 Million Posts Say, Cleaning the Chaos.

于混沌中梳理    百万帖文之声。

Topic modelling 1M+ Facebook posts on contested public issues — with Prof. Sabrina Nardin.

在 1M+ Facebook 帖子上进行主题建模 —— 与 Sabrina Nardin 教授合作。

Built scalable Python ETL pipelines to ingest, clean, and restructure 1M+ unstructured Facebook posts from SQL databases. Standardised noisy text and metadata into model-ready formats; applied BERTopic and LDA for topic discovery, iteratively refining taxonomies to improve labelling consistency. Documented data quality and pipeline decisions throughout to support reproducibility.

构建可扩展的 Python ETL 流程，从 SQL 数据库中提取、清洗并重构 100 万余条 Facebook 帖子。将噪声文本与元数据标准化为模型可用格式；应用 BERTopic 与 LDA 进行主题发现，并迭代精炼分类体系以提升标注一致性。全程记录数据质量与流程决策以支持复现。
